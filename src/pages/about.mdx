# About

My name is Alice (Xinyun). I'm a software engineer in Melbourne. I build developer tools at Zendesk during the day. The rest of the time I'm somewhere between these things:

**Code** — I write it for a living, but I also write it to understand how things work. Most of what I build is for myself first, then maybe for others.

**Keyboards** — I build tiny ones and type on them too much. The sound matters. The layout matters. It's a whole thing.

**Fonts** — I collect them the way some people collect vinyl. I'll change my editor font and feel like a different person.

**Photography** — I take photos of things that won't stay still. Quiet moments, mostly.

**French** — I learn it badly but with great enthusiasm. The typing game on this site is how I practice.

**Notes** — I believe in writing things down. 好记性不如烂笔头 — a bad pen is better than a good memory. My grandma said it to my mom, my mom said it to me. Now it's in my code.

This site is my digital garden. A place where I think out loud, connect ideas, and write things down before they slip away. Nothing here is polished. That's the point — the mess is where the thinking happens.
